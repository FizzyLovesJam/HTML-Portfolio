My Favourite Movies of all time

My Top 3 Favourite Movies By Liam Kennedy

Home Alone

A Family Christmas movie that will fill your heart with joy

UP

A Pixar made animation movie about a friendship between two completely different people as they set adrift a flying house, this movie will be sure to pull at your hear strings.

American Psycho

A Dark Comedy Thriller Set in New York City about a man who feels no emotions except Greed & Disgust. His bloodlust spills into his days and he becomes more violent by the second. You will be questioning what is real and what is fake.
